Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

Still is built on a simple principle: your peace should be private. We don't collect, store, or share any personal data. Period.

Ethical Data Handling

Still does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or telemetry. The application functions entirely offline and on-device. Your quiet moments are your own.

No Accounts

Still does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information. Your identity is irrelevant to your calm.

No Tracking

We do not utilize any analytics services, crash reporting, advertising networks, or third-party SDKs that aggregate data. There are no tracking pixels or scripts monitoring your choices.

Hardware Integration

Still interacts with system features like Dynamic Island and Now Playing. These interactions are mediated entirely by iOS on your local hardware. No interaction data is recorded or transmitted.

Patronage Transactions

Subscriptions and purchases (Still+) are handled securely by Apple. We do not have access to your credit card details, email, or Apple ID. Standard App Store privacy protocols apply.
