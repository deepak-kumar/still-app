Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

Still is built on a simple principle: your peace should be private. We don't collect, store, or share any personal data. Period.

What We Collect

Nothing. Still does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or telemetry. The app works entirely offline and on-device.

No Accounts Required

Still does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use the app. Your experience is completely anonymous.

No Tracking

We do not use analytics services, crash reporting, advertising networks, or third-party SDKs that collect data. Your usage remains between you and your device.

Local Storage & Hardware

Still stores your preferences (like your default scene and breathing mode) locally on your device. Any interactions with system features like Dynamic Island or Now Playing are handled entirely by the iOS operating system. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when the app is uninstalled.

In-App Purchases

If you choose to support Still through a Still+ patronage, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or any personal details.

Children's Privacy

Still does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. The app is designed for general audiences and contains no features that would collect personal data.
