Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

Still is built on a simple principle: your peace should be private. We don't collect, store, or share any personal data. Period.

What We Collect

Nothing. Still does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or telemetry. The app works entirely offline and on-device.

No Accounts Required

Still does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use the app. Your experience is completely anonymous.

No Tracking

We do not use:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Crash reporting services
  • Advertising networks
  • Third-party SDKs that collect data

Local Storage & Hardware

Still stores your preferences (like your default scene and breathing mode) locally on your device using standard iOS storage. Any interactions with system features like Dynamic Island or Now Playing are handled entirely by the iOS operating system and do not involve data collection. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not backed up to our servers (we don't have any)
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app

In-App Purchases

If you choose to support Still through an in-app purchase (Still+), the transaction is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or any personal details. Apple's privacy policy governs these transactions.

Children's Privacy

Still does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is designed for general audiences and contains no features that would collect personal data.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Given our commitment to not collecting data, we don't anticipate significant changes.
